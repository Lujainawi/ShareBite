Welcome to ShareBite

Our Mission:
At ShareBite, our mission is to reduce food waste and strengthen community ties by creating a simple and respectful way for people to share extra food.

Who We Are:
We’re two student developers who care deeply about food justice and community. We created ShareBite as part of a university project, hoping to make a real-world impact — helping people share what they have and find what they need.

How It Works:

  • Have extra food? Donate it so someone else can enjoy it.
  • Need a meal? Find what's nearby and pick it up easily.
  • Want to help? Volunteer to deliver food to those in need.

Together, we turn leftovers into lifelines. 💛

Donate Food

Thank you for choosing to share your extra food!

Your donation helps reduce waste and brings people together. Here’s how it works:

  • 🥇 Step 1: Sign up or log in securely.
  • 📝 Step 2: Add your donation details: food type, description, expiry, and a picture.
  • 📍 Step 3: Share it! Your post will appear on the map for others to find.
