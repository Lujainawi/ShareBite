Welcome to ShareBite

Our Mission:
Reduce food waste and strengthen community ties by creating a simple and respectful way for people to share extra food.

Who We Are:
We’re two student developers who care deeply about food justice and community. ShareBite is part of a university project with real-world impact.

How It Works:

  • Have extra food? Donate it so someone else can enjoy it.
  • Need a meal? Find what's nearby and pick it up easily.
  • Want to help? Volunteer to deliver food to those in need.

Together, we turn leftovers into lifelines. 💛
